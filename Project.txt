-Think about a social network idea.
-Register the domain name.
-Design the layout of the social network.
-Create a sign up, sign in and forgot password pages.
-Create a profile page.
-Profile picture upload option.
-Posting images and videos option.
-Tagging users on posts.
-Adding hashtags to the posts.
-Following users.
-Create a feed where you can view the posts of the followings.
-Liking posts.
-Setting up push notifications for liking, following and tagging users.
-Make app to be installed on devices.
-Writing privacy policy, terms of use and cookies.
